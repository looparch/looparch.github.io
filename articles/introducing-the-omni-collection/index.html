Introducing: The Omni Collection

Endless Knot’s Aim is Simple.

Create the highest quality rugs at every price point to elevate Interior Design, from corporate to retail, to hospitality.

Endless Knot honors centuries-old traditions with their custom Tibetan hand-knotting process while catering to modern time-frames with additional on-demand options.

Featuring the Omni Collection

The Design is in the Details. If your project’s timeline doesn’t allow for a custom hand-knotted rug but you’re still looking for a piece to steal the show, the Omni Collection is the perfect solution.
